## `<idea>` (optional) [low priority]

**Purpose:** Miscellaneous story idea or detail

Each idea represents a specific, meaningful story element that doesn't fit into standard categories (locations, scenes, choices, etc.). Should be concrete enough to use but flexible enough to adapt.

**When creating:**
Give each idea a unique, descriptive name. Write 1-3 sentences describing what makes the idea meaningful to the story and how it could enhance the narrative. Keep descriptions concise but specific.
